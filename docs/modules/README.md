# Fee Management System - Complete Documentation Structure

This directory contains comprehensive documentation for all 10 modules of the Fee Management System.

## Module Structure

### 1. Fee Structure Setup
Configure all fee parameters and rules before the academic year begins
- 6 submodules covering fee heads, templates, assignments, concessions, optional fees, and installment plans

### 2. Fine & Penalty Management
Automate and manage late payment penalties
- 5 submodules covering fine rules, grace periods, exemptions, waivers, and adjustments

### 3. Fee Collection & Receipts
Process payments and generate receipts across all payment modes
- 8 submodules covering receipts, partial payments, advances, multiple payment modes, challans, cancellations, reprints, and refunds

### 4. Online Payment Gateway
Manage online payment integration and monitoring
- 5 submodules covering gateway configuration, real-time monitoring, failed transactions, reconciliation, and payment links

### 5. Reconciliation & Settlement
Match collections with bank statements and ensure accuracy
- 6 submodules covering cash, bank, cheque, gateway reconciliation, discrepancy investigation, and day-end settlement

### 6. Defaulter & Dues Management
Track and manage students with pending dues
- 6 submodules covering defaulter identification, reminders, escalation, academic holds, payment plans, and legal notices

### 7. Fee Reports & Analytics
Generate comprehensive reports for decision-making
- 8 submodules covering collection, due, payment history, head-wise, concession, summary, comparative, and forecasting reports

### 8. Audit & Compliance
Maintain audit trails and ensure regulatory compliance
- 6 submodules covering transaction logs, cancellation audits, concession audits, user activity, change history, and compliance reports

### 9. Notifications & Communication
Automate parent communication regarding fees
- 7 submodules covering due reminders, payment confirmations, receipt delivery, overdue notifications, announcements, templates, and delivery tracking

### 10. Utilities & Administration
System configuration and bulk operations
- 8 submodules covering control center, year rollover, bulk operations, import/export, transaction history, custom reports, and backup/restore
